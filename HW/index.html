Balanced homework  means happy students!

All teachers know that many students hate homework and always complain about having a lot of homework from their other classes. But homework is a compulsory part of studying, so we came up with a concept of a homework balance tool. This app includes:

  • Which courses your class are participating in
  • Course difficulty
  • Amount of homework>
  • Current homework load
  • Interaction between teachers
